## 1. Visual Identity Reference | Referência de Identidade Visual

**EN:** All visual production — apparel, digital, print, signage, event materials — follows the ALL ELITE Visual Identity Manual (VIM v1.0, May 2026). No designer, sponsor, vendor, or production partner applies the brand without first receiving the VIM. The summary below is a reference checklist, not a replacement for the VIM.

**PT-BR:** Toda produção visual — vestuário, digital, impresso, sinalização, materiais de evento — segue o Manual de Identidade Visual da ALL ELITE (VIM v1.0, Mai 2026). Nenhum designer, patrocinador, fornecedor ou parceiro de produção aplica a marca sem receber o VIM primeiro. O resumo abaixo é uma lista de verificação de referência, não um substituto para o VIM.

### Locked decisions | Decisões travadas

**Color palette / Paleta:**
- Primary background / Fundo primário: `#0A0A0A` (pure black)
- Brand signature / Assinatura da marca: `#B89732` (Military Gold / Ouro Militar — Pantone 124 C ref.)
- Secondary canvas / Canvas secundário: `#4A5D23` (Military Olive — garments and field banners only)
- Support / Suporte: `#4A4A4A` (Steel Gray) · `#FFFFFF` (White)
- Forbidden / Proibidas: maroon, red, pink, purple, orange, bright yellow, all greens other than Military Olive

**Typography / Tipografia:**
- Display: Anton (Google Fonts — headlines, wordmark, apparel)
- Body: Inter (Google Fonts — paragraphs, product copy, UI)
- Numerals: Big Shoulders Display (Google Fonts — tier patches, dates, counts)
- Labels: JetBrains Mono (specs, captions, technical labels)

**Logo:**
- Original mark — ring + 3 chevrons + star. Maps 1:1 to the 3 Tier Structure.
- 4 approved color versions: Gold/Black (primary), White/Black, Gold/Olive, Black/Gold (reverse)
- Clear space: minimum equal to the width of the star element on all sides
- Minimum size: 20px digital / 12mm print
- Never rotate, distort, recolor outside the 4 approved versions, or add effects

**Symbols / Símbolos:** 8 original elements — chevron, star, wing, ring, bar, triangle, cross, spear. No skulls, flames, camo, US flags as base graphic, eagles, or official military insignia.

**Language system / Sistema de idiomas:**

| Layer | Rule | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Identity | English only — never translated | "Discipline Above All," "Recruit," "Operator," "Sergeant," taglines |
| Story | Bilingual — English + Portuguese | Founder bio, origin, manifesto |
| Relationship | Portuguese primary | Instagram captions, WhatsApp, product descriptions |

## 2. Training Program | Programa de Treinamento

**EN:** Every session follows the same structural arc. The names change. The structure does not.

**PT-BR:** Cada sessão segue o mesmo arco estrutural. Os nomes mudam. A estrutura não.

### Session structure | Estrutura da sessão

| Time | Segment |
|---|---|
| 0:00–0:05 | Formation, roll call & mission briefing / Formação, chamada e briefing da missão |
| 0:05–0:12 | Warm-up / Aquecimento |
| 0:12–0:20 | Movement prep / Preparação de movimentos |
| 0:20–0:45 | Main mission or individual test / Missão principal ou teste individual |
| 0:45–0:55 | Finisher / challenge / Finalizador / desafio |
| 0:55–1:00 | Cool down, debrief & close / Volta à calma, debrief e encerramento |

### Session types | Tipos de sessão

**EN:**
- **Team mission days:** No Man Left Behind, Casualty Evac, Ammo Resupply, Log Brotherhood Carry
- **Individual days:** Selection Day, Gut Check, The Standard, Combat Fitness Test
- **Strength & carry days:** sandbags, ammo cans, tires, stretchers, logs, bars, weighted vests
- **Saturday Crucible:** higher-intensity public session — R$80–R$150 per person

**PT-BR:**
- **Dias de missão em equipe:** No Man Left Behind, Casualty Evac, Ammo Resupply, Log Brotherhood Carry
- **Dias individuais:** Selection Day, Gut Check, The Standard, Combat Fitness Test
- **Dias de força e carregamento:** sacos de areia, ammo cans, pneus, macas, troncos, barras, coletes
- **Saturday Crucible:** sessão pública de maior intensidade — R$80–R$150 por pessoa

### Programming principle | Princípio de programação

**EN:** Every session has a name, a purpose, and a debrief. It is not random exercise. The mission framing is not aesthetic — it changes how members approach the work and talk about it after.

**PT-BR:** Cada sessão tem nome, propósito e debrief. Não é exercício aleatório. O enquadramento de missão não é estético — muda como os membros abordam o trabalho e falam sobre ele depois.

## 3. Trial Class & Conversion System | Sistema de Aula Experimental e Conversão

**EN:** The acquisition funnel has four steps. Each step has a specific action and a specific time window. Nothing is left to chance.

**PT-BR:** O funil de aquisição tem quatro etapas. Cada etapa tem uma ação específica e uma janela de tempo específica. Nada é deixado ao acaso.

### The four steps | As quatro etapas

**Step 1 — The trial / A experimental**
- One free trial class per person — no exceptions, no repeat freebies
- Before participation: liability waiver, health questionnaire, emergency contact, photo/video authorization
- Instructor Kemp leads the session as normal — no special treatment for trials

**Step 2 — The follow-up / O follow-up**
- WhatsApp message within 24 hours of the trial class
- In Portuguese. Personal, not templated.
- Ask one question: "Qual foi a parte mais difícil?"

**Step 3 — The offer / A oferta**
- Option A: Join within 48 hours → discount on Recruit shirt or waived enrollment fee
- Option B: R$50 trial week (2 sessions) → credited in full to first month if they join

**Step 4 — The close / O fechamento**
- Monthly plan framing: "The drop-in is R$60. The plan is R$297 for the whole month. The math is obvious."
- Never chase. One follow-up after the offer expires.

### Pricing reminder | Lembrete de preços

| Offer | Price |
|---|---|
| First trial class | Free |
| Drop-in | R$50–R$70 |
| Trial week (2 sessions) | R$50 (credited to month 1) |
| Basic plan 2x/week | R$297/month |
| Standard plan 3x/week | R$347–R$397/month |
| Unlimited / Premium | R$497/month |

> **EN:** Drop-in rates must stay high enough that the monthly plan is obviously the better value. If members feel they can drop in cheaply whenever they want, the plan loses its pull.

> **PT-BR:** Os valores de avulsa devem ser altos o suficiente para que o plano mensal seja claramente a melhor escolha. Se os membros sentirem que podem entrar barato quando quiserem, o plano perde força.

## 4. Awards & Member Retention | Prêmios e Retenção de Membros

**EN:** The program must reward more than the top athlete. Loyalty, consistency, grit, and leadership are visible and recognized. This makes more members feel seen and creates reasons to stay beyond their own progress.

**PT-BR:** O programa deve premiar mais do que o melhor atleta. Lealdade, consistência, garra e liderança são visíveis e reconhecidas. Isso faz mais membros se sentirem vistos e cria razões para ficar além do próprio progresso.

### Awards | Prêmios

| Award | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Top Performer | Best physical result of the cycle |
| Most Improved / Mais Evoluiu | Greatest measurable progress |
| Best Battle Buddy / Melhor Companheiro | Most helpful to other members |
| Never Quit | Pushed through the hardest moment — did not stop |
| Iron Attendance | Consistency and discipline over time |
| Mission First | Consistently put the team before the ego |

### Format | Formato

**EN:** Awards are announced at the end of each training cycle (monthly or per competition phase). The ceremony is short — 5 minutes. The recognition is public. Patches, Instagram features, and leaderboard spots are the primary vehicles. Cash or heavy prizes come later, funded by sponsors once the program has scale.

**PT-BR:** Prêmios são anunciados ao final de cada ciclo de treinamento (mensal ou por fase de competição). A cerimônia é curta — 5 minutos. O reconhecimento é público. Patches, destaque no Instagram e posições no leaderboard são os veículos principais. Prêmios em dinheiro ou de alto valor vêm depois, financiados por patrocinadores quando o programa tiver escala.

### Retention principle | Princípio de retenção

**EN:** Most incentives must be low-cost and high-status. A patch costs R$15. A public callout on Instagram costs nothing. A tier promotion ceremony costs a few minutes and creates content that runs for weeks. The goal is to make staying feel better than leaving — not to buy loyalty with discounts.

**PT-BR:** A maioria dos incentivos deve ser de baixo custo e alto status. Um patch custa R$15. Um destaque público no Instagram não custa nada. Uma cerimônia de promoção de nível custa alguns minutos e gera conteúdo que roda por semanas. O objetivo é fazer com que ficar pareça melhor do que sair — não comprar lealdade com descontos.

## 5. Sponsor Activation Playbook | Playbook de Ativação de Patrocinadores

**EN:** A sponsor must receive more than a logo. The activation plan below ensures every partner has a clear, documented experience before, during, and after each event. This is the primary reason sponsors renew.

**PT-BR:** Um patrocinador deve receber mais do que um logo. O plano de ativação abaixo garante que cada parceiro tenha uma experiência clara e documentada antes, durante e depois de cada evento. Essa é a principal razão pela qual patrocinadores renovam.

### Activation timeline | Linha do tempo de ativação

| Phase | Action |
|---|---|
| 3 weeks before / 3 semanas antes | Announcement reel tagging sponsor, story countdown, event page live |
| 1 week before / 1 semana antes | Dedicated reel or story feature of sponsor product/service |
| Event day / Dia do evento | Banner placement, vendor booth or product station, mic mentions during briefing and awards, filmed sponsor moment |
| Within 48h after / Em até 48h depois | Thank-you reel, photo package sent to sponsor (10+ usable images), performance report (reach, views, attendance) |
| 2 weeks after / 2 semanas depois | Recap post tagging sponsor, final performance report |

### Creative activation options | Opções criativas de ativação

**EN:**
- Co-branded challenge — sponsor names a workout station or a specific challenge within Operation Beachhead
- Product test day — sponsor provides product for all participants to use/sample during training
- Exclusive workout — sponsor-named session with their branding on the event day photo/video
- Member discount code — ongoing benefit for ALL ELITE members, tracked and reported monthly

**PT-BR:**
- Desafio co-branded — o patrocinador nomeia uma estação ou desafio específico dentro da Operation Beachhead
- Dia de teste de produto — o patrocinador fornece produto para todos os participantes usarem/experimentarem durante o treino
- Treino exclusivo — sessão com nome do patrocinador e branding deles no foto/vídeo do dia do evento
- Código de desconto para membros — benefício contínuo para membros da ALL ELITE, rastreado e reportado mensalmente

### Sponsor report template | Template de relatório para patrocinadores

Send after each activation. / Enviar após cada ativação.

- Date and event name / Data e nome do evento
- Total attendance / Presença total
- Instagram reach on sponsor posts / Alcance no Instagram nos posts do patrocinador
- Reel views / Visualizações dos reels
- Story reach / Alcance dos stories
- Photo package link / Link do pacote de fotos
- Notable moments / Momentos de destaque
- Next activation date / Data da próxima ativação

> **EN:** Better sponsor experience = higher chance of renewal. The report is not bureaucracy — it is the main reason a sponsor writes the check again next year.

> **PT-BR:** Melhor experiência para o patrocinador = maior chance de renovação. O relatório não é burocracia — é a principal razão pela qual um patrocinador assina o cheque de novo no ano seguinte.

## 6. Online Presence & Content | Presença Online e Conteúdo

**EN:** The brand lives primarily on Instagram and WhatsApp. All content follows the VIM visual standard: black backgrounds, Military Gold accents, Anton headlines, real training environments. No studio. No influencer aesthetics.

**PT-BR:** A marca vive principalmente no Instagram e WhatsApp. Todo conteúdo segue o padrão visual do VIM: fundos pretos, acentos em Ouro Militar, títulos em Anton, ambientes de treino reais. Sem estúdio. Sem estética de influencer.

### Content cadence | Cadência de conteúdo

| Type | Frequency | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Training clip / Clipe de treino | Daily (mobile) / Diário (celular) | Reel or story — 15–60s |
| Founder content / Conteúdo do fundador | 2–3x/week | Reel or post — mission briefings, standards, culture |
| Tier promotion | When earned / Quando conquistado | Post + reel — ceremony moment |
| Product drop | Per launch | Post + reel + countdown story |
| Professional production | 1–2x/month | Full session reel — edited, color-graded, drone if budget allows |
| Competition countdown | 8 weeks out | Daily story as event approaches |

### Photography direction | Direção de fotografia

**EN — Do:**
- Sand, beach, real Vila Velha environment — no gym interiors
- Visible sweat and real effort — not posing
- Hard light or golden hour — high contrast, deep black
- Diverse real members — not a casting

**EN — Don't:**
- Studio backgrounds (white, grey, neutral)
- Models posing with forced smiles
- Excessive vintage filter that corrupts the gold
- Conventional gym equipment in frame
- Product on a generic table — use black background or real field environment

**PT-BR — Fazer:**
- Areia, praia, ambiente real de Vila Velha — sem interiores de academia
- Suor visível e esforço real — sem posação
- Luz solar dura ou golden hour — alto contraste, preto profundo
- Membros reais e diversos — sem casting

**PT-BR — Não fazer:**
- Fundos de estúdio (branco, cinza, neutro)
- Modelos posando com sorriso forçado
- Filtro vintage excessivo que corrompe o dourado
- Equipamento de academia convencional em cena
- Produto sobre mesa genérica — usar fundo preto ou ambiente de campo real

### Language per channel | Idioma por canal

| Channel | Language |
|---|---|
| Instagram caption / Legenda | Portuguese primary, English for brand identity elements |
| Instagram bio | "Discipline Above All" (EN) + Portuguese description |
| WhatsApp | Portuguese — conversational, not formal |
| Website | Bilingual — English for identity layer, Portuguese for all descriptions |
| Online store | Portuguese primary |

### Portuguese-speaking assistant scope | Escopo do assistente em português

- Instagram captions, DMs, story replies
- WhatsApp follow-ups and scheduling

- Sponsor reports (formatting, sending)
- Basic site updates

## 7. Professional Media Plan | Plano de Mídia Profissional

**EN:** Professional media is not used at every regular class. One good production session generates 3–4 weeks of content. Full media teams are reserved for competitions and key sponsor activations.

**PT-BR:** Mídia profissional não é usada em toda aula comum. Uma boa sessão de produção gera 3–4 semanas de conteúdo. Equipes completas de mídia são reservadas para competições e ativações-chave de patrocinadores.

### Budget reference | Referência de orçamento

| Service | Expected cost |
|---|---|
| Basic session with videographer | R$300–R$800 |
| Good part-time videographer | R$800–R$1.500 |
| Videographer with editing | R$1.200–R$2.500 |
| Basic drone session | R$800–R$1.500+ |
| Full drone/video day | R$1.500–R$3.500+ |
| Event full media package | R$3.500–R$10.000+ |

### Production priorities | Prioridades de produção

1. Operation Beachhead — full package including drone, multi-camera, edited highlights
2. Tier promotion ceremonies — capture the moment, edit a short reel
3. Monthly production session — planned training day with videographer, generates bulk content
4. Product drop content — clean product shots on black background, worn in field
5. Daily mobile — handled by Instructor Kemp or assistant, no budget required

### Content repurposing | Reaproveitamento de conteúdo

**EN:** One 3-minute event highlight reel = 4–6 30-second clips = 8–12 story cuts = 2 posts + 4 reels. Always brief the videographer on the priority shots before the session: Instructor Kemp commanding, member faces under effort, tier promotion moment, sponsor branding in frame.

**PT-BR:** Um highlight de 3 minutos = 4–6 cortes de 30 segundos = 8–12 cortes para stories = 2 posts + 4 reels. Sempre passar ao videomaker as prioridades antes da sessão: Instructor Kemp em comando, rostos dos membros sob esforço, momento de promoção de nível, branding do patrocinador em quadro.

**EN:** Squad culture grows when members feel they belong to something that exists outside the training. The goal is to make the community as strong as the program itself.

**PT-BR:** A cultura de esquadrão cresce quando os membros sentem que pertencem a algo que existe fora do treinamento. O objetivo é fazer a comunidade ser tão forte quanto o próprio programa.

### Community actions | Ações de comunidade

| Action | Purpose | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Free public workshop / Workshop público gratuito | Attract new leads, build local credibility | Quarterly / Trimestral |
| Social event (off-training) / Evento social | Bond outside the beach — increases retention | Monthly / Mensal |
| Online challenge / Desafio online | Keep members active between sessions, generate UGC | Per cycle / Por ciclo |
| Tier promotion ceremony | Turn earned progress into public status and content | When earned |
| Member testimonial campaign | Social proof — real transformations, real voices | Rolling / Contínuo |
| WhatsApp community group | Real-time communication, accountability, culture | Always active / Sempre ativo |

### WhatsApp group norms | Normas do grupo WhatsApp

**EN:**
- Used for training reminders, schedule updates, and culture content
- No spam, no unsolicited selling
- Instructor Kemp posts mission briefings before key sessions
- Members share their own results — encouraged but not forced
- New members are welcomed publicly by Instructor Kemp on their first day

**PT-BR:**
- Usado para lembretes de treino, atualizações de agenda e conteúdo de cultura
- Sem spam, sem venda não solicitada
- Instructor Kemp posta briefings de missão antes de sessões-chave
- Membros compartilham seus próprios resultados — encorajado, mas não forçado
- Novos membros são recebidos publicamente pelo Instructor Kemp no primeiro dia

## 9. Legal, Safety & Control | Legal, Segurança e Controle

**EN:** This section covers operational compliance. The goal is to protect the program, the members, and the founder's control of the brand.

**PT-BR:** Esta seção cobre conformidade operacional. O objetivo é proteger o programa, os membros e o controle do fundador sobre a marca.

### Pre-training documentation | Documentação pré-treino

Every participant, every session:
- Liability waiver / Termo de responsabilidade
- Health questionnaire / Questionário de saúde
- Emergency contact / Contato de emergência
- Photo/video authorization / Autorização de foto/vídeo

First-time participants complete all four before joining any session.

### Beach operations | Operações na praia

- Verify local public beach regulations annually
- Confirm requirements for: noise, tents, vendors, temporary structures
- For competitions: formal permit process, first aid team on-site, check-in system, clearly marked safe zones

### Safety protocol | Protocolo de segurança

- Warm-up is mandatory — never skipped
- All movements are coached before loaded
- First aid kit present at every session
- Instructor Kemp has current first aid certification
- Emergency plan document reviewed with any assisting staff before events

### Insurance | Seguro

Start with a basic liability policy once the program reaches 20+ regular members. Reassess at 50 members. Event-specific coverage required for Operation Beachhead.

### Brand ownership | Propriedade da marca

Instructor Kemp retains full, exclusive ownership of:
- Brand name "All Elite" / "ALL ELITE TRAINING"
- Logo mark and all visual identity files (VIM v1.0)
- Instagram @allelite.br
- Website domain www.allelite.com.br
- Online store and all digital platforms
- All client data and contact lists
- All sponsor agreements
- All bank accounts

No partnership, investment, or service agreement transfers or shares ownership of any of the above. Operational support (assistant, sponsor rep, event staff) is contracted or commissioned — never equity-based unless explicitly agreed in writing.

### Contracts | Contratos

Written contracts required for: sponsors, vendors, event staff, production partners, and any recurring service provider. Verbal agreements are not binding for commercial relationships.

## 10. Risks & Mitigation | Riscos e Mitigação

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Weather disrupts training / Clima interrompe treino | High / Alta | Backup session schedule, rain policy communicated to members in advance, future indoor partner location identified |
| Member injury / Lesão de membro | Medium / Média | Waivers, health screening, mandatory warm-up, movement coaching before load, first aid kit on-site, insurance analysis at 20+ members |
| Copycats enter market / Cópia da marca no mercado | Medium / Média | VIM protects visual identity; veteran authenticity and community are not copyable; trademark filing recommended at 50+ members |
| Language barrier limits Kemp's reach | Medium / Média | Portuguese-speaking assistant handles all relationship-layer communication; Instructor Kemp leads training and brand voice; his authenticity in English is the differentiator, not a liability |
| Brand dilution from production errors | Medium / Média | VIM v1.0 distributed to all production partners before any work begins; no asset produced without VIM sign-off |
| Sponsor hesitation — early stage | High / Alta | Start with product/voucher prize sponsors (zero cash risk); build documented proof (reports, photos, reach); cash sponsors come after the first event |
| Overspending on prizes | Medium / Média | All large cash prizes funded by sponsors and registrations — never from operating budget; early awards are patches, public recognition, and tier promotions |
| Slow member growth | Medium / Média | Increase trial class volume, strengthen local partnerships (CrossFit, BJJ, running groups), boost referral incentives, review content cadence |
| Investment terms conflict with operations | Low / Baixa | Instructor Kemp maintains majority control; no investor receives operational authority; all terms documented in writing before funds transfer |
| Equipment damage or theft | Low / Baixa | Secure storage, inventory checklist after each session, insurance rider for equipment once program reaches scale |
